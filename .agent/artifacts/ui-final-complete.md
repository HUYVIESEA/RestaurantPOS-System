```css
Success: linear-gradient(135deg, #4CAF50, #388E3C);
Primary: linear-gradient(135deg, #0060C0, #0078D4);
```

---

## 🎓 WHAT WE LEARNED

1. **Consistency is Key**: Applying same patterns everywhere creates professional look
2. **!important for Headers**: Necessary to override specificity conflicts
3. **Dark Mode**: Plan from start, easier than retrofitting
4. **Gradients**: 135deg angle creates consistent diagonal gradients
5. **Hover Effects**: Keep under 0.3s for snappy feel
