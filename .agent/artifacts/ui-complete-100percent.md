## 🎓 WHAT WE LEARNED

1. **Consistency is Everything**: Applying same patterns creates professional look
2. **!important Usage**: Sometimes necessary for headers to override specificity
3. **Dark Mode Planning**: Easier when planned from start
4. **Gradient Angles**: 135deg creates consistent diagonal feel
5. **Animation Speed**: Under 0.3s feels snappy, over feels sluggish
6. **Semantic Colors**: Makes UI intuitive and predictable
7. **Mobile First**: Design for small screens first, expand up
8. **Component Patterns**: Reusable patterns save time and ensure consistency
